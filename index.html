About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Yi Yang. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2025, advised by Prof. Defu Lian, and my Bachelor's degree from Fuzhou University in 2020.

I am always happy to discuss trustworthy AI, privacy, LLMs, and collaboration opportunities. You can reach me at zhihaozhu@ust.hk.
