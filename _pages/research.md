<section class="research-intro">
  <p class="home-eyebrow">Research agenda</p>
  <h1>Trustworthy AI systems, from recommender models to LLMs.</h1>
  <p>
    My work studies how machine learning systems expose, retain, and misuse training data, and how we can audit these risks in realistic model and application settings.
  </p>
</section>

<section class="home-section">
  <h2>Research Interests</h2>
  <div class="focus-grid">
    <article class="focus-card">
      <h3>Data privacy and auditing</h3>
      <p>User data revocation, membership inference, ownership verification, and training data detection.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="focus-card">
      <h3>LLM privacy and exposure</h3>
      <p>Methods for identifying pre-training data and measuring privacy leakage in language and vision-language models.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="focus-card">
      <h3>Robust learning systems</h3>
      <p>Stress testing graph, recommender, and AI-for-finance systems against adversarial and model stealing attacks.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
</section>
